# Retail-Consumer-Behavior-Analytics

Retail Consumer Analytics is an end-to-end data analytics project that explores customer purchasing behavior using transactional retail data. The project focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful business insights through data preprocessing, SQL-based analysis, and interactive dashboard visualization.

The workflow demonstrates a complete analytics pipeline from data cleaning to business intelligence reporting.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing patterns across multiple product categories.
- Understand the impact of demographics on purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate customer segmentation and subscription trends.
- Generate business insights to support strategic decision-making.
- Build an interactive dashboard for data visualization.

---

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- SQL
- Power BI

---

## Project Workflow

### Data Preparation (Python)

- Imported and explored the dataset using Pandas.
- Performed data cleaning and handled missing values.
- Standardized column names for consistency.
- Engineered new features including customer age groups and purchase frequency.
- Validated data quality and removed redundant attributes.
- Loaded the cleaned dataset into PostgreSQL for analysis.

### Business Analysis (SQL)

Executed SQL queries to answer key business questions, including:

- Revenue distribution by gender
- High-value customers using discounts
- Highest-rated products
- Shipping method comparison
- Subscriber vs. non-subscriber purchasing behavior
- Customer segmentation based on purchase history
- Product performance by category
- Revenue contribution across age groups

### Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Developed an interactive dashboard to visualize:

- Customer demographics
- Sales performance
- Revenue trends
- Subscription analysis
- Product category insights
- Customer segmentation
- Purchase behavior

---

## Key Insights

- Customer demographics influence purchasing behavior and revenue generation.
- Subscription status is associated with higher customer engagement and spending.
- Express shipping customers tend to spend more per transaction.
- Customer segmentation enables more targeted marketing strategies.
- Product ratings and discount utilization help identify sales opportunities.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase subscription adoption through exclusive benefits.
- Strengthen customer loyalty initiatives.
- Optimize promotional and discount strategies.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
- Promote top-performing products to maximize revenue.
